Support & Help Center

Everything you need to know about using CFA Ethix

Welcome to the CFA Ethix Support page. This guide will help you get the most out of the app and resolve any issues you may encounter.

1. Getting Started

1.1 System Requirements

  • Platform: iOS 17.0 or later
  • Device: iPhone or iPad
  • Internet Connection: Required for AI-powered features and syncing

1.2 Creating an Account

  1. Open the CFA Ethix app
  2. Tap "Sign Up" on the authentication screen
  3. Enter your email address and create a secure password
  4. Tap "Sign Up" to create your account
  5. You're ready to start practicing!

1.3 Signing In

If you already have an account:

  1. Open the app and tap "Sign In"
  2. Enter your email and password
  3. Tap "Sign In" to access your account

2. Key Features

2.1 Home Dashboard

Your central hub for CFA Level 2 Ethics practice:

  • Standard Cards: Browse all 7 CFA Ethics standards (Standard I through VII)
  • Daily Practice Goal: Track your daily progress with the practice counter
  • Quick Access: Tap any standard card to start practicing questions on that topic

2.2 Study Mode

Practice with AI-generated vignettes and questions:

  • Realistic Scenarios: Each problem includes a detailed vignette mimicking the CFA exam format
  • Multiple Choice Questions: Select from options A, B, or C
  • Instant Feedback: See if your answer is correct immediately
  • Detailed Explanations: Understand why the correct answer is right
  • Bookmark Problems: Save questions for later review by tapping the bookmark icon

2.3 AI Tutor (Chat Feature)

Get personalized help on any problem:

  • Tap the "Chat with Tutor" button while viewing a problem
  • Ask specific questions about the vignette, options, or ethical principles
  • Receive contextual explanations powered by DeepSeek AI
  • The tutor has full context of the current problem you're working on

2.4 Saved Problems

Review your bookmarked questions:

  • Access the "Saved" tab to see all bookmarked problems
  • Tap any saved problem to review it
  • Swipe to Delete: Swipe left on any problem to remove it from your saved list
  • All saved problems sync across your account

2.5 Settings & Account Management

  • View Profile: See your account email and user ID
  • Sign Out: Log out of your account
  • Delete Account: Permanently remove your account and all data

3. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: How are the practice questions generated?
A: All questions are generated in real-time by DeepSeek AI, tailored to the specific CFA Level 2 Ethics standard you've selected. Each vignette is unique and designed to test your understanding of ethical principles.
Q: Can I practice offline?
A: An internet connection is required to generate new problems and use the AI Tutor feature. However, you can review previously saved problems offline once they've been loaded.
Q: How does the daily practice goal work?
A: The app tracks how many problems you've completed each day. This counter is stored locally on your device and resets at midnight. It helps you maintain consistent practice habits.
Q: What happens when I delete my account?
A: All your data, including saved problems, practice history, and account information, will be permanently deleted from our servers. This action cannot be undone.
Q: Can I use the app on multiple devices?
A: Yes! Your account syncs across all your iOS devices. Saved problems and account information are stored in the cloud, so you can access them from any device where you're signed in.
Q: Why is a problem taking long to generate?
A: Problem generation typically takes 3-10 seconds. If it's taking longer, check your internet connection. If the problem persists, try closing and reopening the app.
Q: Is CFA Ethix affiliated with the CFA Institute?
A: No, CFA Ethix is an independent study tool created to help candidates prepare for the CFA Level 2 Ethics section. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by the CFA Institute.
Q: How accurate are the AI-generated questions?
A: Questions are generated based on the official CFA Ethics standards and are designed to closely mimic the exam format. However, always cross-reference with official CFA Institute materials for your primary study.

5. Privacy & Data

5.1 What Data Is Collected?

CFA Ethix collects minimal data to provide its services:

  • Account information (email, encrypted password)
  • Saved/bookmarked problems
  • Your answer selections
  • AI interaction data (prompts and responses)

For complete details, please read our Privacy Policy.

5.2 How to Delete Your Data

  1. Open the app and navigate to Settings
  2. Scroll down to find "Delete Account"
  3. Confirm the deletion
  4. All your data will be permanently removed from our servers

6. Tips for Effective Practice

  • Set Daily Goals: Aim to complete at least 3-5 problems per day
  • Cover All Standards: Practice questions from all 7 Ethics standards, not just one or two
  • Review Explanations: Always read the detailed explanation, even when you answer correctly
  • Use the AI Tutor: Don't hesitate to ask questions when you're unsure about a concept
  • Bookmark Challenging Problems: Save difficult questions to review before your exam
  • Understand, Don't Memorize: Focus on understanding the underlying ethical principles
